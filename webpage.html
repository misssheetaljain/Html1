BTIRT has always had a global outlook. It was the first to internationalize its reach to promote the youth of Bundelkhand. Renowned personalities have been a part of the institute.
Babulal Tarabai (BT) group of Educational Institutions, under registered society LSES (Little Star Education Society, founded in 1993). This group consists of “BTIRT (Babulal Tarabai Institute of Research and Technology 2008), BTIE (Babulal Tarabai Institute of Excellence2006), BTIPS (Babulal Tarabai Institute of Pharmaceutical Science 2019), BTM (Babulal Tarabai Mahavidyalaya 2019) and LSSM (Little Star Shailesh Memorial Senior Secondary School Sagar 1992)”, progressing under the chairmanship of visionary Mr. Santosh Jain ‘Ghari’. Little Star Education Society was founded with a pious objective of providing quality education for the benefit of society and the nation as a whole. In the year of formation of education society, it established “Little Star Shailesh Memorial Senior Secondary School providing education to children from Nursery to 12th standard.


Babulal Tarabai Institute of Research and Technology (BTIRT) is a private engineering college located at Sironja in Sagar district of Madhya Pradesh. It was established by Little Star Educational Society, Sagar in 2008. It is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Technological University Bhopal. College offers undergraduate degree in engineering in five disciplines, a MBA program and MCA program. All the courses are approved by AICTE and DTE Govt. of MP. It is also an ISO 9001:2008 Certified Institution The mission of the BTIRT is to be one of the leading technical institutes disseminating globally acceptable education, effective industrial training and relevant research output Departments: BTIRT has following academic departments- Department of Computer Application Department of Business Administration Department of Electronics & Communications Engineering Department of Mechanical Engineering Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering Department of Computer Science and Engineering Admission Procedure: Admissions to undergraduate and post graduate courses are governed by DTE, Govt. of MP through entrance exam After the entrance examination DTE organizes off campus counseling session for the allotment of seats. Placement: BTIRT has a well-functioning training and placement cell which organizes continuous training sessions, industrial visits and on campus placement drives for BTIRT students. Some of the main recruiters that has collaboration for placement with the college are- Airtel, Concierge Technologies, JIBJIT, Sonalika International, Dreamz –I etc.
Facilities: BTIRT provides all the necessary facilities to the engineering students. Some of them are mentioned below-

  • Library: Large number of books, periodicals, video cassettes and CD-ROMs are available in the BTIRT library. The library is fully computerized and CDNET facilities are to be developed very soon. Library has also been subscribed to access all AICT approved journals and magazines.
  • Hostels: BTIRT provides girls’ hostel with accommodation capacity of 40 students, all rooms are well furnished and provided all the basic requirements of pro education environment. The hostel is having 24/7 internet facility with computers.
  • Scholarship: The College provides following scholarships to the eligible students-
  • Government of India scholarships
  • Government of MP Scholarships
  • BTIRT Special Scholarships (Adhoc merit grants).
